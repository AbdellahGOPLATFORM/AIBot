How Does This Work?


The idea behind this app is to make the recruitement process easy, not just for the recruiter, but also the applicant. Our ChatBot shortlists the applicants on the basis of a test conversation. The applicant is supposed to answer a set of questions posed by the ChatBot and on the basis of that gets a score. The applicant receives this score and is informed whether they are selected or not with a set of feedback for future.
